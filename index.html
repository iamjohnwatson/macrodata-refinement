About Macrodata Refinement

This is a tribute project inspired by the TV show "Severance" on Apple TV+.

Created by Akash Sriram as a fan recreation of the Macrodata Refinement (MDR) department interface.

How to Play:

  • Select connected groups of identical numbers
  • Drag them to any of the five bins
  • The numbers have different "tempers" based on their value:
    • 0: Dread
    • 1: Malice
    • Even numbers (2, 4, 6, 8): Woe
    • Other numbers (3, 5, 7, 9): Frolic
  • All bins accept all tempers
  • Complete the file by filling all five bins to 100%

No affiliation with Apple TV+ or the creators of Severance.
